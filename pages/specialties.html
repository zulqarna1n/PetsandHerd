Our Specialties

General Practitioner

At General Clinic, we offer the essential basis for the health of your pet. Our dedicated veterinarians provide consultations regular vaccinations and preventive exams. We are committed to providing a long and healthy life for the your partner, taking care of their general health needs.

General Practitioner

Nephrology

In our Nephrology and Urology team, we specialize in diagnose and treat kidney and urological problems. We perform kidney function tests, we treat urinary infections and we ensure that your pet receives specific care to maintain your urinary health in perfect condition.

Nephrology

Orthopedic

In our orthopedic specialty, we treat your pet's musculoskeletal problems. We perform orthopedic surgeries, treat fractures, and manage conditions such as arthritis. Our goal is to restore mobility and improve your pet's quality of life.

Orthopedic

Cardiology

At Veterinary Cardiology, we diagnose and treat heart disease. We use advanced techniques to monitor your pet's cardiovascular health, ensuring their heart is in optimal condition for a full life..

Cardiology

Nutritionist

Our specialized nutritionists create personalized meal plans to meet your pet's specific needs. We promote a balanced diet to support their overall health, providing nutritional support tailored to their individual needs..

Nutritionist

Dermatologia

In Veterinary Dermatology, we address skin and coat problems. If your pet is dealing with allergies, infections, or other dermatological issues, our team is here to offer effective solutions and ensure comfort and well-being. Diagnostic Imaging: In our Diagnostic Imaging specialty, we use advanced technologies to diagnose internal conditions. X-rays and ultrasound allow for accurate assessments, aiding in efficient diagnoses so we can provide the best possible care.

Dermatologia

Radiologia

In our Diagnostic Imaging specialty, we take a modern and advanced approach to understanding your pet's internal health. Using technologies such as x-rays and ultrasound, our highly qualified team offers an in-depth and accurate look inside your pet's body.

Radiologia

Neurology

At Veterinary Neurology, we treat neurological disorders with special attention. If your pet is experiencing problems such as seizures or paralysis, our highly qualified team uses advanced techniques to diagnose and treat them, prioritizing your pet's quality of life and comfort..

Neurology

Why should I take my pet to a specialized veterinarian?

Taking your pet to a specialized veterinarian is essential for targeted care and accurate diagnoses. Specialized professionals have in-depth knowledge, offering personalized treatments to ensure the health and quality of life of your pet. This specific care helps identify and address health problems effectively, ensuring a long and healthy life for your pet. At Pets & Herd, we are committed to providing this exceptional care, relying on trained specialists and advanced technology to ensure the health and quality of life of your pet..
