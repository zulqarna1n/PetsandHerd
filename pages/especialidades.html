Our Specialties

General Practitioner

Our General Practice service provides the essential foundation for your pet’s health. We offer regular check-ups, vaccinations, and preventive exams. Our goal is to ensure your companion has a long, healthy life with proper overall care.

General Practitioner

Nephrology

Our Nephrology and Urology team specializes in diagnosing and treating kidney and urinary tract problems. We perform renal function tests, treat urinary infections, and provide specialized care to maintain your pet’s urinary health.

Nephrology

Orthopedics

In Orthopedics, we treat musculoskeletal problems in pets. We perform orthopedic surgeries, treat fractures, and manage conditions such as arthritis. Our mission is to restore mobility and improve your pet’s quality of life.

Orthopedics

Cardiology

Our Veterinary Cardiology service diagnoses and treats heart diseases. Using advanced techniques, we monitor your pet’s cardiovascular health to ensure a strong heart and a longer, healthier life.

Cardiology

Nutrition

Our specialized Nutritionists design personalized diets to meet your pet’s unique needs. We promote balanced nutrition to support overall health, ensuring tailored care for each individual.

Nutrition

Dermatology

Our Veterinary Dermatology team treats skin and coat problems. If your pet suffers from allergies, infections, or other dermatological issues, we provide effective solutions to restore comfort and well-being.

Dermatology

Radiology

Our Imaging Diagnosis specialty uses modern technologies such as X-rays and ultrasound to evaluate your pet’s internal health. This allows accurate, detailed assessments for effective treatment plans.

Radiology

Neurology

Veterinary Neurology treats nervous system disorders with special care. If your pet suffers from seizures, paralysis, or other neurological problems, our team uses advanced techniques to diagnose and treat while ensuring comfort and quality of life.

Neurology

Why should I take my pet to a specialized veterinarian?

Visiting a specialized veterinarian is essential for targeted care and accurate diagnoses. These professionals have in-depth knowledge and can provide personalized treatments to ensure your pet’s health and quality of life. At VetPet, we are committed to offering exceptional care with qualified specialists and advanced technology, ensuring the best for your companion.
